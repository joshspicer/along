# Design system

## North star: Morning Window

Along feels like two people checking in at a bright kitchen window: clear,
hopeful, and personal without sentimentality. Neutral surfaces dominate.
Saturated color always identifies a person or session state.

## Semantic palette

| Token | Light | Dark | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Greenhouse teal | `#0B6B61` | `#61C7B8` | Primary action, self, shared thread |
| Warm coral | `#B94F46` | `#FF9C91` | Partner identity, restrained success |
| Window sun | `#A66B00` | `#F5C65B` | Open room, arrival, completion |
| Clear daylight | `#F7FAF9` | — | Light background |
| Night glass | — | `#101816` | Dark background |
| Soft graphite | `#17201E` | `#EDF5F2` | Primary text |

Status also uses labels, icons, and shapes; color is never the sole signal.
Body text targets at least 7:1 contrast on the main background.

## Type, space, and motion

Use the platform humanist sans family, large readable type, tabular numerals
for clocks, and one obvious action per screen. Resting cards are flat with
tonal layering and borders rather than shadows. Motion responds to state only,
never blocks a task, and becomes effectively immediate when reduced motion is
enabled.

## Core motifs

- Two softly rounded, overlapping person shapes form the Along mark.
- A broad circular timer is the Focus home’s visual center.
- Teal identifies “you”; coral identifies the paired person.
- Window sun is reserved for a room opening, a partner joining, or a genuine
  completion.
- No decorative gradients, glassmorphism, nested cards, or ornamental
  animation.
