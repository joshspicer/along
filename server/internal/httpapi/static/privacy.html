Along privacy

Along is a private focus space for two people. We use account, passkey-public-credential, pair, session, optional note, device, and notification-token data only to provide the service.

We do not upload contacts or biometrics, show advertising, sell data, build public profiles, or track activity across apps and websites.

Security and notifications

Passkey private keys remain on your credential provider. Recovery and refresh tokens are stored as hashes by the service. Notification device tokens are encrypted, and notification copy contains no names or note text.

Your choices

Notifications are optional. You can revoke passkeys, sessions, and devices in Along. For access or deletion requests, email privacy@along.app.
