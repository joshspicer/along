# Product contract

## Purpose

Along makes showing up together intimate and effortless. Either person can
start an ad-hoc 25-minute focus session in one tap. Their paired partner sees
the already-running room and may join without an invitation, waiting room, or
clock reset. Finished solo and shared sessions become a simple, newest-first
chronology.

## Primary navigation

Only two destinations are primary:

1. **Focus** — start, discover, join, pause, resume, finish, cheer, or leave a
   short note.
2. **Look back** — remember completed sessions without scoring them.

## Product rules

- Pairing is private, one-time, and belongs to stable accounts rather than
  installations.
- Presence communicates “here,” never activity surveillance.
- Offline sessions remain solo and sync later; an offline room is never
  joinable.
- Server timestamps are authoritative for shared sessions.
- Push notification delivery is helpful but never required for correctness.
- Notification permission is contextual and optional.
- There are no habits, schedules, feeds, follower mechanics, scores, streaks,
  leaderboards, relationship ratings, or guilt-based copy.

## Voice and accessibility

The voice is warm, direct, and quiet. Along meets WCAG 2.2 AA, supports large
Dynamic Type, screen readers, keyboard and switch access, reduced motion,
automatic light/dark themes, color-independent state, and targets of at least
48 logical pixels.
