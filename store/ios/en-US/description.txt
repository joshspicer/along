Along is a private place for two people to make a little focused time.

Start a 25-minute focus in one tap. Your paired person can see the room already
in progress and join the same clock without an invitation or waiting screen.
Finish on your own or together, leave an optional small note, and find completed
sessions newest-first in Look back.

Along has no feeds, scores, streaks, schedules, public profiles, or relationship
ratings. Notifications are optional and limited to useful partner moments.
Passkeys protect your self-hosted account without a password.
